• The Hermitian complex n-by-n matrices do not form a vector space over the complex numbers, ℂ, since the identity matrix In is Hermitian, but i In is not. However the complex Hermitian matrices do form a vector space over the real numbers ℝ. In the 2n2-dimensional vector space of complex n × n matrices over ℝ, the complex Hermitian matrices form a subspace of dimension n2.
